AMINO BLOGGING ASSISTANT

for bloggers on amino apps.

1 | text alignment

Select the alignment of your text.

Left-alignment
Center-alignment

2 | text styles

Select the style options for your text (leave all unchecked for none).

Italic [I]
Bold [B]
Underline [U]
Strikethrough [S]

3 | indentation

Enter how many spaces you want at the start of each line as indentation (0 for none).

4 | custom start

Enter any custom, special characters that you want to appear after the indentation but before the text, including spaces (leave blank for none).

your line style preview:

The field below updates as you modify the style settings above so you can preview how each line of text will be formatted.

5 | max. line characters

Enter the maximum number of characters that you want per line (minimum 15), or check the box below if you do not want to divide your text.

No line division.

6 | your blog

Multiple paragraphs are supported! Enter each paragraph separated by a space. Then, press the 'Format text' button below to format your text with the style settings above.

your formatted blog:

Once you press 'Format text', your formatted blog will appear below. Press the 'Copy text' button to copy all of your formatted text onto your clipboard.
